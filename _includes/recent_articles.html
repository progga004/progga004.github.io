About Me


Hi, I'm Progga Paromita Dutta from Bangladesh, currently residing in the USA. I am passionate about technology and data science, and I am excited to explore new opportunities in this dynamic field.

I began my undergraduate journey as a transfer student at Stony Brook University in Spring 2022, where I am currently a senior, double majoring in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and Statistics. I am set to graduate in December 2024. Prior to my studies at Stony Brook, I pursued Mechanical Engineering at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), which laid the foundation for my analytical and problem-solving skills.

Beyond academics, I am actively involved in campus life as a ballroom dancer and a member of the Stony Brook University Ballroom Team. I have competed in prestigious competitions at Harvard and Tufts. Additionally, I am a proud member of Women in Computer Science (WiCS) and the Stony Brook Computing Society (SBCS), where I engage with a vibrant community of peers who share my enthusiasm for technology and innovation.

I was particularly fascinated by the intersection of AI and the metaverse, and the ways in which virtual reality and AI can combine to create immersive and engaging experiences.
